Academic research

For the decade prior to becoming an entrepreneur (2002—2012), I enjoyed the lifestyle of a curious academic. My research in this period was inspired by a range of applications in biophysics, and focused on continuum field theory and numerical algorithms. This page links to descriptions of my work, and serves as a catalogue of my scientific writing, presentations and research software.
